1. Business Understanding

Objective:
Dream Housing Finance aims to automate the loan eligibility process based on customer details provided in their online application form. The company currently operates across urban, semi-urban, and rural areas and wishes to quickly identify customer segments eligible for loans, thereby targeting them more effectively.

Problem Statement:
The company wants to automate the loan eligibility process based on various customer attributes such as:

Gender
Marital Status
Education
Dependents
Income (Applicant and Coapplicant)
Loan Amount
Credit History
Property Area
The goal is to predict if a customer is eligible for a loan based on these details.
